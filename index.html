Intro

Most people call me Paul. I work at Amazon, where I apply automated reasoning techniques on industrial problems. I have a PhD from University College London (UCL) under Prof. Byron Cook. I collaborated with Oracle Labs, and Amazon AWS during my PhD. In my former life, I was a software engineer @ Optiver.
