Intro

Most people call me Paul. I work @ Amazon as a Senior Applied Scientist, where I apply automated reasoning techniques to large scale industrial problems. I am currently working on automating the contract driven development process by leading the development of the Coastguard tool among many other projects. I have a PhD from University College London (UCL) under Prof. Byron Cook. Before Amazon I worked at AWS where I started and co-developed Tiros, a tool that analyses Ec2 networks for security vulnerabilities. Before that, I spent almost a year at Oracle Labs where I worked on Souffle with my collegues Prof. Bernhard Scholz and Herbert Jordan. In my former life, I was a software engineer @ Optiver, Toshiba R&D and CSIRO.
