Intro

Most people call me Paul. I work @ Amazon, where I apply automated reasoning techniques to large scale industrial problems. I have a PhD from University College London (UCL) under Prof. Byron Cook. I collaborated with Oracle Labs, and Amazon AWS during my PhD where I started and co-developed Tiros, a tool that analyzers Ec2 networks for vulnerabilities. Before That I spent almost a year at Oracle Labs where I worked on Souffle with my collegues Bernhard Scholz and Herbert Jordan. In my former life, I was a software engineer @ Optiver and Toshiba R&D.
